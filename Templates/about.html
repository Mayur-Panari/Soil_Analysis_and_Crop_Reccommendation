Soil Analysis and Crop Recommendation

Our mission is to empower farmers with real-time soil analysis and accurate crop recommendations using machine learning models, ensuring sustainable agriculture and high-yield crops.

We are student of computer science with a passion for agricultural technology. Our goal is to combine our knowledge of machine learning with the needs of the farming industry to create innovative solutions that improve crop yield and farming efficiency and best crop reccommendation.

The machine learning model uses historical data, such as soil nutrients ,ph and Rainfall, to provide accurate recommendations for the best crop choices for each farmer's soil conditions.

We use LogisticRegresion model for the finest accuracy. The model's accuracy is constantly being evaluated and improved through ongoing feedback from users. With continuous learning from new data, it achieves an accuracy of over 96% in crop recommendation.
